Our Mission at SmokeLite

SmokeLite was founded with a single goal: to empower people to take control of their health by quitting smoking.

We combine technology, behavioral science, and human compassion to create a personalized quit journey that’s effective and sustainable. Whether you're just starting or need support after a relapse, we're here for you.
